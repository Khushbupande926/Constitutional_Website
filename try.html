About us

A story of Best

Nagrik Navigators is an innovative digital platform aimed at spreading constitutional awareness among citizens. By simplifying complex legal content and transforming it into interactive games and activities, we make learning about the Constitution engaging and fun. With support for multiple Indian languages, augmented reality experiences, and blockchain-based certification, our mission is to empower every citizen with knowledge of their rights and duties in an accessible, inclusive way.

Latest Features

Quizzes

Short, interactive quizzes test users' understanding of constitutional concepts and reinforce learning. Immediate feedback helps users improve their knowledge in real-time.

Chatbot

An AI-powered chatbot answers user queries about the Constitution, providing explanations and guidance in simple terms, making learning more accessible.

Certificates

Blockchain-based certificates are awarded for completing modules, ensuring that users' achievements are verifiable and secure.

Badges

Users earn badges for completing specific tasks or mastering certain constitutional topics, motivating continuous learning.

Milestones

Progress milestones mark key achievements in the learning journey, encouraging users to stay motivated and track their growth.

Trophies

Special trophies are awarded for outstanding performance, such as high quiz scores or completing all game levels, enhancing user engagement.
